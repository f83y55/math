Fractions

Comprendre les règles :
Cocher le bon résultat.
\(\dfrac{-3}{8}\times(-2) = \)
\(\dfrac{3}{4}\)
 □ \(\dfrac{-5}{8}\) □ \(\dfrac{6}{16}\) □ \(\dfrac{3}{4}\)
\(\dfrac{2}{3}-\dfrac{5}{6} = \)
\(\dfrac{-1}{6}\)
 □ \(\dfrac{3}{18}\) □ \(\dfrac{-1}{6}\) □ \(\dfrac{-5}{9}\)
\(\dfrac{4}{7}\times\dfrac{4}{3} = \)
\(\dfrac{16}{21}\)
 □ \(\dfrac{8}{21}\) □ \(\dfrac{3}{7}\) □ \(\dfrac{16}{21}\)
\(\dfrac{5}{8}+\dfrac{3}{4} = \)
\(\dfrac{11}{8}\)
 □ \(\dfrac{5}{96}\) □ \(\dfrac{5}{6}\) □ \(\dfrac{15}{32}\)
L'inverse de \(\dfrac{5}{12}\) est
\(\dfrac{-12}{5}\)
 □ \(\dfrac{-5}{12}\) □ \(\dfrac{-12}{5}\) □ \(\dfrac{12}{5}\)
Le carré de \(\dfrac{-9}{4}\) est
\(\dfrac{81}{16}\)
 □ \(\dfrac{3}{2}\) □ \(\dfrac{81}{4}\) □ \(\dfrac{81}{16}\)
\(\dfrac{7}{6}\pi\times(-3\pi) = \)
\(\dfrac{-7}{2}\pi^2\)
 □ \(\dfrac{-7}{2}\pi^2\) □ \(\dfrac{-7}{3}\pi^2\) □ \(\dfrac{-7}{3}\pi\)
\(\dfrac{30\,000+100}{100} = \)
\(\dfrac{3}{4}\)
 □ \(30\,000\) □ \(30\,001\) □ \(301\)
Appliquer
Donner le résultat sous la forme d'une fraction irréductible (que l'on ne peut pas simplifier).
